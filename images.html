SPATT: Towards High-fidelity Garment Transfer with Spatial-aware Texture Transfer

Ting Liu 1, Jianfeng Zhang 2, Xuecheng Nie 2, Yunchao Wei 3, Shikui Wei 1, Yaozhao 1, Jiashi Feng 2

1Beijing Jiaotong University, 2National University of Singapore, 3University of Technology Sydney

Given an image of a target person and a model image with the desired clothing, we address the problem of transferring the desired garment to the target person. To this end, we propose a novel two-stage SPatial-Aware Texture Transfer (SPATT) framework. Compared with existing frameworks as shown experimentally, the proposed framework can generate more realistic images with better preserved texture details. Furthermore, difficult cases where two persons have large pose and view differences can also be well handled by SPATT.
